Hello! I'm Minyoung Kim. I am currently a undergraduate student at <a href="https://www.korea.edu/">Korea University</a> and a research intern of <a href="https://alinlab.kaist.ac.kr/">ALIN Lab</a> at <a href="https://www.kaist.ac.kr/">KAIST</a>, advised by <a href="https://alinlab.kaist.ac.kr/shin.html">Prof. Jinwoo Shin</a>. 

My research goal is to develop a general-purpose AI system that understands the physical world and interacts with
it. I am particularly interested in building general world models that maintain internal and temporal consistency
and leveraging such models to enable interaction with the environment. I am also open to exploring emerging
research areas that could contribute to the development of more general AI systems.
